About Rooster Joe

Delicious coffee. Housemade food. Cozy spot.

Every roster has its own personality. So does every coffee roast and fresh menu item at Rooster Joe. We're Coldwater. Ohio's coffee shop that offers quality beans behind every cup, plus daily housemade soups, salads, sandwiches, and more - all served quickly in a relaxing space that's meant for everyone. Come for a quick break. Catch up with a friend. Hit up our drive-thru when you're on the go. We'll be there to welcome you and give you the fuel you need to find your crow.

Our mission

We provide exceptional coffee, food, and customer service in a welcoming atmosphere, so our neighbors have everything they need to comfortably socialize, rest, or work.

About our owners

Meet Michelle and Joe.

Over 20 years ago, newly married Coldwater, Ohio natives, Michelle and Joe McClurg, began dreaming of how they would create the perfect coffee shop. With their combined love of quality coffee and espresso, and a healthy invigoration meal (Joe and Michelle help her father on an organic dairy farm, and Michelle is a registered dietitian/foodie). They imagined a simplified menu that would perfectly complement a hot (or iced) cup of something special. Fast forward to 2019, and Rooster Joe is here. While they're committed to being on-trend. Michelle and Joe value friendliness, simplicity, quality, and speed over everything else - something fresh and tasty, made in record time, in a welcoming space that feels like home. You'll find them behind the counter. They want to meet you, get to know you, and serve you again and again.
